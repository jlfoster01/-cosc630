The animals at Allegany County Animal Shelter are in DANGER and the shelter needs your Help!

The shelter is in danger of becoming a kill shelter again.

Kill shelters euthanize healthy and treatable animals when they have reached capacity and can no longer accept new animals.

Why is this happening?

County Commissioners do not want to support the no-kill shelter budget the shelter needs to run and care for Allegany's County's animals in need.
